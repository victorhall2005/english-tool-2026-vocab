## What EnglishTool Is

EnglishTool is a practical browser-based companion for growing English vocabulary through active use. Instead of relying on single-pass memorization, it lets you record words, attach meanings and examples, and come back to them with review cycles that reinforce retention.

It is aimed at learners who prefer a compact tool that matches real reading and listening sessions. With word capture, transcript extraction, and listening drills, EnglishTool works well for study based on articles, media, or structured vocabulary lists.

## Capabilities

- Create vocabulary entries with definitions and sample sentences
- Automatically populate items with dictionary and translation data
- Bulk import many entries to speed up setup
- Pull vocabulary from transcripts and study materials
- Review with spaced repetition study modes
- Train with listening drills and shadowing-style exercises
- Export and import study data, including sync code support
- Choose between dark and light themes for easier reading
- Capture words directly through Chrome extension workflows

## How to Use It

A common workflow looks like this:

1. Add a word manually or capture it from a webpage.
2. Enter the definition, translation, and example usage.
3. Place items into a review list.
4. Study them with spaced repetition until they feel familiar.
5. Move on to listening drills or shadowing practice for audio reinforcement.
6. Export your progress when you need a backup or a transfer point.

Example study flow:

- Capture a new term while reading
- Auto-fill the meaning and translation
- Review it later with repetition mode
- Practice it again through listening
- Sync or import the set on another session

## FAQ

**How do I update EnglishTool?**  
Swap your local files with the latest build from the project source, then reopen the app in your browser.

**Where are my settings stored?**  
Settings and study data live in the browser environment or move through the app's export/import flow, depending on how you use it.

**Can I move my vocabulary to another device?**  
Yes. Use the export/import tools or sync code support to transfer your study set.

**What should I do if capture or auto-fill is not working?**  
Check browser permissions, confirm the Chrome extension setup if you are using capture from Chrome, and make sure the page content can be accessed for extraction.

**Does it support both reading and listening practice?**  
Yes. EnglishTool includes vocabulary review, listening drills, and shadowing-oriented practice.
